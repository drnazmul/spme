The Pharma Civil War: R&D vs. QC

Within the same building, there is a divide. R&D teams ("The Detectives") use SPME to solve complex impurity mysteries. QC teams ("The Gatekeepers") avoid it, preferring older "dilute and shoot" methods due to regulatory fear.
